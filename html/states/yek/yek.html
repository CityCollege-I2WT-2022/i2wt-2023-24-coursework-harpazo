Demonocracy of Yek

Spawn of Darkness

The Yek are a race of big demonborn creatures, who are always hungry for flesh and intercourse.

Legend has it that the Yek emerged from the depths of an infernal abyss, their origins entwined with the very fabric of darkness and malevolence. As they clawed their way into the mortal realm, their demonic visages struck terror into the hearts of those unfortunate enough to witness their arrival. With horns that pierced the heavens and eyes that gleamed with an unholy light, the Yek were destined to become harbingers of torment.
