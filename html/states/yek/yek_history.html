History

They used to rule the entire continent with a tyrannical fist, treating the other races there as literal livestock. They are also very hungry for war and never back down from a fight, even going as far as to invade the Mov Empire at its peak.

Nowadays they are a former shell of themselves, with a series of revolutions supported by nearby powers overthrowing their grasp on the island.
