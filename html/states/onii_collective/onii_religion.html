Onii Collective

Religion

Beyond the reverence for the Anima, the Onii's spiritual beliefs delve into the realms of animism. Each Eldertree is seen as a living conduit to the spirit world, and the Onii believe that the rustle of leaves and the creaking of branches are messages from the Anima. Ritualistic dances beneath the sacred trees are accompanied by melodic chants, resonating with the belief that the vibrations carry prayers directly to the ethereal realm. The Onii also construct Spirit Stones, unique formations crafted from iridescent crystals, believing them to amplify the connection between the physical and spiritual realms. Sacred ceremonies involve the lighting of Lumifires, enchanted flames that flicker in colors corresponding to the presence of different spirits.

As the sun sets over the Onii Collective, a celestial transformation bathes the land in a soft, otherworldly glow. This celestial embrace, known as the Dusk's Embrace, is believed to be a direct manifestation of the Anima's blessings. During this enchanting hour, the Eldertrees seem to hum with ancient melodies, and the Luminara butterflies twirl in a mesmerizing crescendo. The Onii often gather in communal spaces, sharing stories and songs as the Dusk's Embrace wraps every corner of their realm in a serene, celestial luminescence.
